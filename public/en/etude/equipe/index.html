The team

The CIRCAME study is the result of a collaboration between a research team at Inserm, the “EpiAgeing” team, from the Centre of Research in Epidemiology and Statistics, and two clinical teams from Paris hospitals at APHP, the “Centre Mémoire de Ressources et de Recherche” (CM2R) Ile-de-France Paris Nord, and the Geriatric Day Hospital at Bretonneau hospital.

The CIRCAME-EYE study, conducted at Fernand-Widal hospital, is in collaboration with the Ophtalmologic department at the Fondation Adolphe de Rothschild Hospital.
